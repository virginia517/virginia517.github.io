Target-audience

This site is for students, travelers, and workers age 14 above, who wants to get update and keep informed about the weather. Visitors can access this site with desktop or mobile devices.

Personas

  • Tom is a photographer. He shoots photo for different occassions.
  • Amy is a vlogger. She makes video content about different places. She travels a lot.

Scenarios

  • Henry is looking for a good location for his work on this coming week. His chosen location was the place where the typhoon rainfall has landed. He wants to know update about that place.
  • Henry will be shooting prenup wedding photos for his client, so he gets onto my website to see when is the best day to meet them.
  • Amy has a trip to Palawan but it has been raining for few days. She wants to know if she needs to reschedule her flight.
  • Amy is planning to go on hiking to vlog the beauty of the mountain. She wants to know what is the weather is going to be for this week. so she gets on my website to know when is the best time to go.
  • Amy will wants to know what the weather is going to be so that she knowa the appropriate clothes to wear
